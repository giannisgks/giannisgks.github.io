RECIPE APP IN PYTHON

The Recipe App was created as part of our assignment for the "Applied Programming with Python" course during the spring semester of 2023. The idea emerged from the need that we, as students, have to find quick and easy recipes to make. Typically having a specific/limited number of ingredients, the application mainly focuses on everyday recipes. The code is available and free for personal use.

Screenshots

Welcome Screen
This is the first page that the user engages with, getting a first glance of the Recipe App.
INGREDIENTS LIST
After clicking 'Start here, the ingredient list pops up. In this screen, all the user has to do is choose the cooking ingredients that are available in his house. After that, to confirm the selected ingredients he presses the 'Confirm ingredients' button and 'Recipe App' will do the rest for him.
RECIPES LIST
By then clciking 'Show Recipes', the recipes that can be prepared show up. They do so in a button shape, lettin the user press on the prefered recipe and view the portion of each ingredient needed and the method to prepare it.
CHOSEN RECIPE
Once the prefered recipe title is clicked, the user gets the recipe with the exact portion of each ingredient needed and a number of steps to follow, in order to get it done. In every screen the 'go-back' buttons are available for the user's best possible experience.
